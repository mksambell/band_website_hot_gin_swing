Repertoire


Our repertoire includes a wide range of jazz and swing classics by artists such as Nat King Cole, Frank Sinatra, Nina Simone, Julie London, Ray Charles and Michael Buble, as well as many blues, soul and pop songs.

We can play strict tempos for ballroom dance, and we can play swing and jive for lindy hop. We can play easygoing jazz standards, and we can play contemporary songs with a vintage vibe!

We pride ourselves on being able to tailor our set specifically to the needs of the occasion. Need a specific song played? Need background music for drinks? Got a particular request for the first wedding dance? Get in touch for a friendly discussion about how we can help!
