About us


Welcome to Hot Gin Swing!

We are a 6-piece jazz / swing band based in South Wales, featuring some of the regions' finest musicians.

We love to play for dances, parties, weddings, festivals, corporate events...you name it!

We tailor our repertoire and the size of the band to meet the requirements of each event. We play as a duo, trio, quartet, or the full band. We also provide a combination: for example, a piano and bass duet for a drinks reception, followed by the full band for an evening dance.

See the FAQs below for more information and check out the rest of the site for pictures, videos and testimonials. Visit our Facebook page for more info and upcoming gigs.

If you have any further questions or would like to make a booking, don't hestitate to get in touch. We look forward to hearing from you!

FAQs


What style of music does the band play?

Our repertoire is largely jazz and swing, but we also play a range of soul, rhythm and blues, and contemporary pop. Take a look at our Music page for more details.

What combinations of musicians do you offer?

We pride ourselves on being able to offer a flexible range of formats:

  • 2-piece: vocals and piano / vocals, sax and backing tracks / piano and bass / piano and sax
  • 3-piece: vocals, piano and sax or bass / sax, piano, bass
  • 4-piece: vocals, piano, bass and drums / sax, piano, bass and drums
  • Full band: vocals, sax, piano, bass and drums
How long does the band play for?

Our standard booking is 2 x 45 minutes sets, but we are happy to discuss playing for longer or for different time arrangements

How much do you charge?

We offer a bespoke service for each event so we prefer to discuss fees on an individual basis. Get in touch for a quote!

Can you play strict tempo dance music?

Yes! We love to play for dancers! We have tailored sets for a range of dance styles

What does the band wear?

We like to dress smart: dresses, DJs and dickie bows! If you need a less formal look for your event, we can also dress down!
